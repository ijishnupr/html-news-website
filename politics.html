Manipur violence: Army steps in, CM says lack of communication led to inter-tribal clashes

By India Today News Desk: A rally organised by the All Tribal Student Union Manipur (ATSUM) in the Torbung area of Manipur's Churachandpur turned violent on Wednesday night. The rally was organised by the union to protest the demand of Meiteis for Scheduled Tribe (ST) status. Violence broke out during a "Tribal Solidarity March" and the state government suspended internet for five days to tackle the worsening law and order situation in the state.
